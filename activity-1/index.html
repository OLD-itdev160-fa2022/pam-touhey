Matlock: Great TV Show or the Greatest TV Show?

Matlock was an American legal television drama from 1986 to 1995. It starred Andy Griffith as Ben Matlock, a criminal defense attorney often considered the best in Atlanta.

Reasons Why Matlock is the Best

  1. Matlock always wins! (well almost always)
  2. Conrad McMasters is a delight
  3. His private investigator usually going undercover
  4. Despite making $100,000 per case, he's a cheap skate
  5. He calls people 'jackass' a lot

He had a folksy demeanor and rumpled blue(ish)-gray suit. He would take on cases pro bono even though he didn't want to. He always let people know exactly what he was thinking "What's the matter with you? Did someone cut the wire between your brain and your mouth?"

"He couldn't kill anyone. He might bore them to death..."
